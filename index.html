The Concept

Two Worlds.
One Game.

Combine the strategy and bluffing of poker with the unpredictability of real World Cup matches. Your hand strength is decided by the beautiful game.

⚽

Your card strength is determined by REAL World Cup results. The teams you hold are the cards you play. Win big when your teams dominate.

🃏

Strategic Depth

The Bluff Factor

FAQ

Common Scenarios

Everything you need to know about edge cases and special situations.

What if my teams aren't playing today?
+

Your maximum score is 0 points. You're dead in the hand. Folding early is the smart move to save chips for the next round. Or... bluff your way to victory.

What if no one's teams are playing?
+

The round is skipped and the dealer shuffles and deals new cards immediately. Nobody loses chips — it's a free pass to the next hand.

Can multiple players have identical scores?
+

Yes! If two or more players end up with the same total points, they split the pot equally. This happens more often than you'd think when multiple teams draw.

What if both my teams draw?
+

Each team gives 3 points. Your total: 3 + 3 = 6 pts. A solid hand! If multiple players have the same score, they split the pot equally.

Team wins 5-0. What score do I get?
+

5 pts (Win) + 4 pts (3+ goals) + 2 pts (Clean Sheet) = 11 pts total. Your team won, scored 5, AND conceded none. That's near-maximum for a single card!

Team loses 3-4. What score do I get?
+

0 pts (Loss) + 4 pts (3+ goals scored) = 4 pts. Even in defeat, if your team scores 3+ goals, you earn the high-scorer bonus!

How do penalties work?
+

Penalty SCORED: +1 pt. Penalty MISSED: -1 pt. Each penalty applies separately. 2 scored = +2 pts. 1 scored + 1 missed = 0 pts net.

What if I fold?
+

You're out of the hand and lose whatever you bet. However, you keep your remaining chips. Smart folding preserves your stack for better opportunities.
